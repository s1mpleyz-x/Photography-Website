Explore Campus

The School Library

The school library is a quiet place where students can read, study, and access a wide range of books and resources. It is equipped with comfortable seating, computers, and study rooms for group work.
An interesting fact about the library is that it has a special collection of rare books and manuscripts that are available for research purposes.
Students can also participate in various reading programs and workshops organized by the library staff.

The School Cafeteria

Our school cafeteria offers a variety of delicious meals and snacks for students and staff. It is a great place to grab a quick bite between classes and after school.
An interesting fact about the cafeteria is that it sources fresh ingredients from local farms to ensure healthy and tasty options for everyone.
and it is also a great place for the students to socialize and relax during their breaks.

The Football Pitch

The football pitch is a vibrant space where students can engage in friendly matches and physical activities.
It is well-maintained and equipped with proper safety measures to ensure a fun and secure environment for all participants.
The school football team uses it to train and host inter school matches,students can also join the football club to improve their skills and enjoy the sport.

The Computer Lab

The computer lab is a modern facility equipped with high-performance computers and the latest software for students to use for their studies and projects.
It provides a conducive environment for learning and research, with reliable internet connectivity and technical support available.
Students can access online resources, conduct virtual experiments, and collaborate on group assignments in this state-of-the-art space.

The Student Centre

The student centre is a hub for student activities, providing spaces for socializing, studying, and participating in various clubs and organizations.
It hosts events, workshops, and meetings that foster a sense of community and engagement among students.
The centre also offers resources and support services to help students succeed academically and personally.
